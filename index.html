Congressional Support for the Use of Military Force

Welcome to the website presenting the Congressional Support for the Use of Military Force (CSUMF) dataset. This dataset measures sentiment towards the use of force in nearly two hundred crises from the end of the Second World War until today, and provides an overall congressional sentiment score for each crisis as well as sentiment breakdown by political party. The dataset is a superior proxy for measuring congressional sentiment than any other alternative and is a valuable resource for researchers in political science, history, and law.
